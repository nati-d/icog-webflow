My iCog Interview Experience

Stepping into the iCog interview was a challenge that pushed my knowledge across multiple domains. Here’s a breakdown of the topics I tackled:

Front-End Development

The interviewer asked about responsive design, optimizing web performance, and structuring components efficiently. I discussed my experience with React, CSS best practices, and accessibility improvements.

Back-End Engineering

We dove into API design, authentication strategies (JWT & OAuth), and database optimization. I shared insights on building scalable Django applications and using indexing in SQL for performance.

Machine Learning

They quizzed me on model selection, feature engineering, and the trade-offs between bias and variance. I explained my understanding of supervised vs. unsupervised learning and real-world ML applications.

Graph Traversal & DSA

One of the toughest parts! I was given a problem requiring BFS and DFS, testing my ability to choose the right algorithm. I tackled it using Python and optimized the solution for efficiency.

Final Thoughts

The experience was intense but rewarding. Each question made me reflect on my strengths and areas to improve. Whether or not I land the role, this interview has fueled my passion for problem-solving even more!
